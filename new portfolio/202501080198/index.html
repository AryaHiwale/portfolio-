The Avengers

The Avengers are a team of extraordinary superheroes who come together to protect Earth from powerful threats. When danger is too big for one hero, the Avengers unite and fight as a team. They were originally formed in Marvel comics and later became one of the most famous superhero teams in the world through movies produced by Marvel. Their main goal is to maintain peace and defend humanity from enemies like aliens, artificial intelligence threats, and cosmic villains.

Origin

The Avengers were created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby. They first appeared in Marvel Comics in 1963.

Famous Members

  • Iron Man
  • Captain America
  • Thor
  • Hulk
  • Black Widow
  • Spider-Man
